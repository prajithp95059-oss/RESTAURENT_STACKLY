FAQs

Things People Ask

Got questions? We've got answers. If yours isn't here, just call us!

Can I walk in without a reservation?
+
Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome during off-peak hours (weekday lunches and early evenings). However, on weekends and special evenings, tables go fast — booking guarantees your spot and the best seating experience.
How far in advance should I book?
+
For weekday dinners, a day in advance is plenty. For weekends, special occasions, or parties of 6+, we recommend booking 3–5 days ahead. Peak holiday seasons can fill up weeks in advance!
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
+
No problem at all — life happens! Just call us or WhatsApp at least 2 hours before your reservation. We hold tables for 15 minutes, so if you're running late, give us a quick ring.
Do you cater for dietary requirements?
+
Absolutely! Vegan, gluten-free, Jain, and other dietary needs are fully accommodated. Just mention it in your booking or let our staff know when you arrive. Our chefs love the challenge!
Can I bring my own cake for a birthday?
+
Yes! Outside cakes are welcome with a small plating fee. Better yet — let our pastry chef bake you something magical. Just let us know when booking and we'll sort out the surprise.

Still Have Questions?

Call us, WhatsApp us, or just walk in and say hello. We don't bite!
