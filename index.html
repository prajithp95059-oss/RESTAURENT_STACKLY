Our Story

It All Started With A Crazy Hunger!

Back in 2012, our founder was wandering Salem's streets after midnight, desperately hungry, and thought: "Why can't great food be FUN?" That one grumbling stomach changed everything.

Today, Stackly is Salem's happiest restaurant — where bold flavours meet even bolder laughs. From crispy starters to decadent desserts, we stack greatness on every single plate!
