Our Story

ONE HUNGRY NIGHT CHANGED EVERYTHING

Back in 2012, our founder Arjun Krishnan was wandering Salem's streets after midnight, desperately hungry, and thought: "Why can't great food be FUN?"

That one grumbling stomach changed everything. He rented a tiny kitchen, hired two friends, and opened Stackly with just 8 tables and an absolutely unhinged menu. Queues formed on day one.

Today, Stackly is Salem's happiest restaurant — where bold flavours meet even bolder laughs. From crispy starters to decadent desserts, we stack greatness on every single plate!

Our Journey

HOW WE GOT HERE

From a midnight craving to Salem's most beloved restaurant — here's every juicy chapter.

2012
The Crazy Beginning
Opened with 8 tables, 2 chefs, and an absolutely unhinged menu. Salem had never seen anything like it.
2014
First Big Award
Won "Best New Restaurant in Tamil Nadu" at the Regional Food Excellence Awards. We cried. A lot.
2016
Big Expansion
Moved to our current location, tripled seating, added the open kitchen and live music stage.
2019
Chef Priya Joins
Pastry legend Chef Priya joined the team and immediately broke Salem's collective sweet tooth.
2021
Survived & Thrived
Launched delivery, home kits, and cooking classes during tough times. Our community carried us through.
2025
Still Going Wild
12,000+ happy customers, 24 awards, and still the craziest kitchen in Salem. Not stopping anytime soon.
